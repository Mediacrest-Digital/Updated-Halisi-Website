Halisi Family Hospital

Our Culture Statement

At Halisi Family Hospital we conduct and present ourselves professionally, we are competent and continually strive to improve our knowledge and skills through continuous learning . We value integrity and are open and honest with our clients and always strive to do the right thing in the right way at the right time and for the right reasons. We do our very best in each and everything we do to ensure that we always offer excellent service. We treat our patients with warmth, love and compassion and humble ourselves in service to them.

Our Mission

What We Do and Why We Do It

To serve and care for families by providing high-quality health and wellness

Our Vision

What We Want to Achieve

Trusted and Loved by Families

Family - Everyone is family.

Kindness - We genuinely care for our family and go out of our way to serve them.

Integrity - We are open and honest in all our interactions to foster trust, and we do the right thing, in the right way, at the right time and for the right reasons.

Excellence - We are eager to improve knowledge, skills, systems and processes. We value great work, love what we do and hate mediocrity. We pay attention to detail, work hard, and delight in always exceeding expectations.

Respect – We respect and value our family’s opinions, preferences, privacy, time and money.

Dedication - We are dependable, committed to our work and loyal to the organization.

Solutions Oriented - We are not afraid of problems and embrace challenges with a focus on solutions and results.

Our Promise

Quality healthcare you can trust.
